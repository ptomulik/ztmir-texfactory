\section{Wstęp}
\label{sec:LBU9I}

Właściwości dynamiczne liniowych układów o stałych skupionych można określić
m.in. na podstawie odpowiednich charakterystyk zarówno w dziedzinie czasu, jak
też dziedzinie częstotliwości. W pierwszym przypadku często wykorzystuje się
charakterystyki impulsową i skokową natomiast w drugim, charakterystyki
częstotliwościowe (np. wykresy Nyquista lub Bodego). Znajomość transmitancji
operatorowej układu, a co za tym idzie -- transmitancji widmowej, pozwala na
wyznaczenie obu typów charakterystyk. W związku z tym zachodzą również
możliwości:
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{itemize}
  \item wyznaczania transmitancji widmowej układu na podstawie charakterystyki
    skokowej,
  \item wyznaczania charakterystyki skokowej na podstawie transmitancji
    widmowej układu.
\end{itemize}

\section{Podstawy teoretyczne}
\label{sec:K5NDO}

Niech $h(t)$ będzie odpowiedzią układu na skok jednostkowy $\bm{1}(t)$.
Istnieje bezpośredni związek między transmitancją operatorową $G(s)$ układu
a~transformatą $H(s) = \mathscr{L}\{h(t)\}$ odpowiedzi skokowej $h(t)$\footnote{%
  Wynika to z~faktu, że $\mathscr{L}\{\bm{1}(t)\} = 1/s$ oraz
  $H(s) = G(s)\cdot\mathscr{L}\{\bm{1}(t)\}$.%
}:
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  G(s) = s H(s).
  \label{eq:SQUJF}
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
Prawa strona równania \eqref{eq:SQUJF} to nic innego jak transformata
$\mathscr{L}$ pierwszej pochodnej funkcji $h(t)$:
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  \begin{aligned}
    & s H(s) = \mathscr{L}\left\{\od{h(t)}{t}\right\}, && h(0) = 0. &
  \end{aligned}
  \label{eq:MFHCT}
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
Korzystając z~definicji transformaty Laplace'a, równanie \eqref{eq:SQUJF} można
zatem zapisać jako:
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  G(s) = \int_0^{\infty}{\od{h(t)}{t} \cdot e^{-s t} dt}.
  \label{eq:L018O}
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
Stąd wprost wynika formuła na transmitancję widmową $G(j\omega) = G(s)|_{s=j\omega}$:
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  G(j\omega) = \int_0^{\infty}{\od{h(t)}{t} \cdot e^{-j\omega t} dt}.
  \label{eq:DYXKC}
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

Podział całki
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  \int_0^{\infty} \od{h(t)}{t} \cdot e^{-j\omega t} dt
    = \int_{t_0}^{t_n} \od{h(t)}{t} \cdot e^{-j\omega t} dt
    + \int_{t_n}^{\infty} \od{h(t)}{t} \cdot e^{-j\omega t} dt
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

Całkowanie przez części
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  \int_{t_0}^{t_n} \od{h(t)}{t} \cdot e^{-j\omega t} dt
    = \left[h(t) \cdot e^{-j\omega t}\right]_{t_0}^{t_n}
    + j\omega \int_{t_0}^{t_n} h(t) \cdot e^{-j\omega t} dt
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

Kwadratura (trapezy), przy założeniu $\Delta t = t_k - t_{k-1}$
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
    \int_{t_0}^{t_n} h(t) \cdot e^{-j\omega t} dt
      \approx \Delta t \left(
          \frac{h_0 e^{-j\omega t_0} + h_n e^{-j\omega t_n}}{2}
        + \sum_{k=1}^{n-1} {h_k e^{-j\omega t_k}}
      \right)
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

Wracamy
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  \int_0^{\infty} \od{h(t)}{t} \cdot e^{-j\omega t} dt
    \approx h_n e^{-j\omega t_n} - h_0 e^{-j\omega t_0}
    + j\omega \Delta t \left(
          \frac{h_0 e^{-j\omega t_0} + h_n e^{-j\omega t_n}}{2}
        + \sum_{k=1}^{n-1} {h_k e^{-j\omega t_k}}
      \right)
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

Po uporządkowaniu wyrazów, otrzymujemy składowe transmitancji
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  \begin{aligned}
    P(\omega) & \approx h_n \cos{\omega t_n} - h_0 \cos{\omega t_0}
      + \omega \Delta t \left(
        \frac{h_0 \sin{\omega t_0} + h_n \sin{\omega t_n}}{2}
        + \sum_{k=1}^{n-1}{h_k \sin{\omega t_k}}
      \right),
    \\
    Q(\omega) & \approx h_0 \sin{\omega t_0} - h_n \sin{\omega t_n}
      + \omega \Delta t \left(
        \frac{h_0 \cos{\omega t_0} + h_n \cos{\omega t_n}}{2}
        + \sum_{k=1}^{n-1}{h_k \cos{\omega t_k}}
      \right).
  \end{aligned}
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

Jeśli charakterystyka $h(t)$ nie jest podana w~postaci analitycznej, lecz
graficznej (np. została zdjęta doświadczalnie), wtedy transmitancję widmową
układu można wyznaczyć w~sposób uproszczony. W~tym celu dokonuje się
aproksymacji uzyskanej krzywej za~pomocą linii łamanej
(rysunek~\ref{fig:Z319K}).
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{figure}[!ht]
  \centering
  \input{lpas10/approx.tex}
  \caption{Zasada aproksymacji charakterystyki skokowej $h(t)$}
  \label{fig:Z319K}
\end{figure}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
Przybliżoną wartość pochodnej funkcji $h(t)$ dla każdego przedziału
aproksymacji określa iloraz różnicowy wprzód:
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  \begin{aligned}
    & B_k = \frac{h_k - h_{k-1}}{t_k - t_{k-1}}, && \text{dla } k=1,2,\dots,n, &
  \end{aligned}
  \label{eq:DPN3S}
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
gdzie wielkości $h_k$ i~$t_k$ oznaczają współrzędne $k$-tego punktu, natomiast
$n$ jest liczbą odcinków linii łamanej.

Zależność \eqref{eq:DPN3S} ma również zastosowanie w przypadku ćwiczenia, gdy
charakterystykę skokową rejestruje się przy użyciu przetwornika
analogowo-cyfrowego (A/C). Wielkości $h_k$ i~$t_k$ oznaczają wtedy współrzędne
$k$-tego punktu pomiarowego a interwały $(t_k-t_{k-1})$ są równe okresowi
próbkowania przetwornika $\Delta t$.

Po~uwzględnieniu \eqref{eq:DPN3S} wyrażenie na transmitancję widmową przyjmie
postać:
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  G(j\omega) = \sum_{k=1}^n{B_k \int_{t_{k-1}}^{t_k}{e^{-j\omega t} dt}}
             = \sum_{k=1}^n{\frac{j B_k}{\omega}\left(
                  e^{-j\omega t_k} - e^{-j\omega t_{k-1}}
                \right)}
  \label{eq:6JRJH}
\end{equation}
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
Po przekształceniach szukaną transmitancję widmową można zapisać w dogodnej
formie do wykreślenia charakterystyki amplitudowo-fazowej (A-F):
%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\begin{equation}
  \begin{aligned}
    P(\omega) & = \frac{1}{\omega} \sum_{k=1}^n{B_k \left(
      \sin{\omega t_k} - \sin{\omega t_{k-1}}
    \right)}
    \\
    Q(\omega) & = \frac{1}{\omega} \sum_{k=1}^n{B_k \left(
      \cos{\omega t_k} - \cos{\omega t_{k-1}}
    \right)}
  \end{aligned}
  \label{eq:Y7Q4P}
\end{equation}
